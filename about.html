About Project Morris

Project Morris is a web-based tool created to help developers more easily identify and research user activity within online groups that share or promote content not suitable for all audiences (NSFW).

By simplifying the process of locating these accounts, the project provides a resource for developers who want to better understand patterns of engagement in such communities.

This project was independently developed by Mater as a personal initiative. It is not sponsored, endorsed, or affiliated with Roblox Corporation in any way. Project Morris exists solely as an informational and technical resource, with the goal of supporting transparency and research within online spaces.
